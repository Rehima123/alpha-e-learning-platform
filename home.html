Ethiopian Freshman Year Structure

📚

First Semester

Common foundation courses: English, Critical Thinking, Math, Geography, Psychology, Physical Fitness, and Physics

🎓

Second Semester

Continuation courses: Advanced English, Anthropology, ICT, Entrepreneurship, History, Civic Education, Global Affairs, Economics, and Inclusiveness

🔬

Natural Science Stream

Focus on Physics, Biology, Chemistry, and Advanced Math - leads to Engineering, Medicine, and Natural Sciences

🌍

Social Science Stream

Focus on Economics, Geography, History, and Communication - leads to Law, Social Sciences, Education, and Business

After Freshman Year

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do I have access to the courses?

+

All courses include 6 months of full access to materials, videos, and resources. Certificates remain accessible for lifetime once earned.

What's the difference between Natural and Social Science streams?

+

Natural Science focuses on Physics, Biology, Chemistry, and Math - leading to Engineering, Medicine, and Sciences. Social Science focuses on Economics, History, Geography, and Communication - leading to Law, Business, and Education.

Can I switch between streams?

+

Stream selection typically happens after completing common freshman courses. Your choice depends on your interests, GPA, and career goals. Consult with academic advisors for guidance.

How does the AI assistant help me?

+

Alpha AI provides instant answers to your academic questions, study tips, exam preparation guidance, career advice, and course recommendations. It's available 24/7 to support your learning journey.

What GPA do I need for competitive programs?

+

Competitive programs like Medicine and Engineering typically require 3.5+ GPA. However, requirements vary by university. Focus on maintaining the highest GPA possible in your freshman year.

Are instructors available for questions?

+

Yes! All courses are taught by experienced Ethiopian instructors. You can ask questions through course forums, and our AI assistant provides instant support anytime.
