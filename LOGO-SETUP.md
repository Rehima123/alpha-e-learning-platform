# Logo Setup Instructions

## How to Add Your Logo

1. **Save your logo image** as `logo.png` in the project root folder:
   ```
   C:\Users\Merkneh\Desktop\alpha tutorial\logo.png
   ```

2. **The logo should be:**
   - PNG format with transparent background (recommended)
   - Approximately 200x200 pixels or similar aspect ratio
   - Clear and readable at small sizes

3. **Your logo will appear:**
   - In the navigation bar (top left)
   - In the footer
   - On all pages automatically
